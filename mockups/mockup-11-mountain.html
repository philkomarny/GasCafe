The Stop

More than a gas station — a Crested Butte institution since 1998. Killer breakfast sandwiches, the best burgers in the Butte, and rock & roll on the stereo.

Perched at 8,885 feet in the heart of Colorado's last great ski town, Gas Cafe One Stop has been fueling adventures (and appetites) for over two decades. Stop in for gas, stay for the food, leave with a story.
