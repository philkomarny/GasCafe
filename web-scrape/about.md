# Gas Cafe One Stop - About

## About Us

"Gas station, killer sandwiches & convenience store all wrapped into one!"

This establishment features an on-site deli offering quality sandwich options for customers.

A notable amenity is the complimentary Mountain Express bus service, which arrives and departs every 15 minutes directly adjacent to the location.
