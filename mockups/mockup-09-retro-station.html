More Than a Gas Station

Gas Cafe One Stop has been fueling up Crested Butte locals and visitors since 1998. What started as a simple filling station has become a beloved community landmark -- the kind of place where the coffee's always hot, the breakfast burritos are legendary, and the vibe is pure mountain rock & roll.

We're at 9,000 feet but we keep it grounded: real food, real people, and a real good time. Whether you're gassing up for a backcountry adventure or grabbing a burger after a long day on the trails, we've got you covered.
