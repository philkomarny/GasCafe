WEBSITE DESIGN MOCKUPS

Eleven design directions for the new Gas Cafe website. Click each one to see the full mockup. Let us know which direction you like — or mix and match elements from different options.

01

Highway to Flavor

Bold horizontal color bands — black, red, blue. Oversized logo. Red pinstripe nav bar. Concert tour poster meets highway billboard.
02

Backstage Pass

All dark with chalkboard texture background. Red as the only accent color. Moody semi-transparent cards. Like the actual Gas Cafe interior — dark tin ceiling, chalkboard menus.
03

Vinyl Record

Circular motifs everywhere — vinyl records, gas pump gauges. Logo inside a record label circle. Rounded cards and badges. 1970s rock album packaging meets vintage gas station.
04

Neon Nights

Pure black background. Everything styled as glowing neon signs — red and blue glow on text, borders, and buttons. Flickering logo. Gas station signs on a dark mountain highway.
05

Americana Station

The clean option. White and cream backgrounds alternating with dark sections. Blue nav bar, red CTAs. Structured grid layout. Modern restaurant site with a rock & roll edge.
06

Apple Clean

Premium minimal design inspired by Apple. Pure CSS with backdrop-blur nav, gradient hero with stats, clean typography, subtle hover effects. No framework — just elegant simplicity with a rock & roll soul.
07

Neubrutalist Diner

Punk zine energy. Hard black borders, flat bold colors, chunky box-shadows, zero border-radius. Tilted cards, sticker motifs. Bright yellow and white backgrounds — nothing dark or polished.
08

Concert Poster

Each section is a full-screen poster with its own color and vibe. Massive dramatic serif type, grain textures, torn edges between sections. Like flipping through gig posters at a record store.
09

Retro Gas Station

Lovingly restored 1960s gas station. Warm cream palette, vintage sign frames, gas pump price displays for menu items, analog gauges, circular badges. Route 66 roadside charm.
10

Swiss Grid

Design magazine editorial. Strict 12-column grid, condensed all-caps typography, almost entirely black and white with red as the only accent. Information-dense, typographically rigorous.
11

Mountain Highway

Warm Colorado mountain lodge feel. Cream and forest green palette, elegant serif headlines, golden CTAs, organic curves, generous whitespace. High-end hospitality energy for a beloved gas station deli.

Pick one direction — or tell us what you like from each.
